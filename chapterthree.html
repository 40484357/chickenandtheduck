Chapter Three: The Duck flys north
As winter dawned, the days got shorter, the winds got stronger, the duck got bored and cold. The duck decided it was time to spread his wings and fly north. He ventured to Portugal and Spain but the heat was too much, so he went to Scotland. It was still cold but warming up, it was friendly, lots of ponds and greenbelts and he was happy.
