Prologue: 4 years later
4 years have passed and the chicken has received her EggsD in Photonics, using lasers to measure the perfect trajectory to get chickens to fly. The duck is studying Webbed Feet Development, hoping to stream line to process and reduce energy and consumption waste for all birds. This unlikely pairing are destined for success but no matter where they go or what they achieve, their greatest success and tale is finding each other because they know that they provide each other with the ultimate happiness.
