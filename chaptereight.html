Chapter Eight: The perfect match
The duck swooned the chicken, shaking his tail feathers, the chicken was beak over talons for him. It was a match made in heaven, a match that would provide the eternal flame that would keep both the chicken and the duck warm for years. A couple dates and a few pecks later The Chicken and The Duck are together and in love. Sometimes birds not of a feather still flock together.
