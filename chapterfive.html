Chapter Five: The Duck gets cold
The duck was settled, his nest was cosy, he had freedom and was earning bills but as the seasons begun to change the duck realised he was getting really cold. The duck didn't want to fly back South, he was happy in the North it was new and exciting. The duck needed a fire and to make a fire he needed some Tinder.
