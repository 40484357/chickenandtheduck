Chapter Six: The Duck meets The Chicken
With tinder in his grasp, the duck just needed some matches to light his fire, swiping through leaves, branches and rubbish all the matches he found were used, or broken. Until he found the chicken, using her quantum nuclear roosting method she had developed the perfect match. The duck asked her for a match and she asked him if he would smoke autumn leaves, to which the duck embarrassed at being caught said yes but very infrequently. The chicken refused but the duck persisted...
