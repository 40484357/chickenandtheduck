Chapter Seven: The Piri Piri Chicken
After many back and forths, constant chirping the chicken gave in. The duck flew to the local Coop Tap and as he laid eyes on the chicken his mind went quackers. He knew this chicken could provide him with heat for life, a flame exploded inside of him, he knew this wasn't an ordinary chicken. During his time in Portugal he had heard of a rare type of Chicken, one that all the animals spoke about but had never met, the Piri Piri Chicken. He knew at that moment, that this was his Piri Piri Chicken.
