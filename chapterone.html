Chapter One: The Duck
Once upon a time there was a Duck who lived in Southern Africa. The duck had been filled with experience, he had seen Elephants, Giraffes, Lions and even Tigers. The Duck had travelled to the warm beaches and windy mountains, he had swum in giant lakes, freezing seas and powerful rivers. The duck had seen and done it all, or so he thought...
