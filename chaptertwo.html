Chapter Two: The Chicken
The Chicken hailed from the freezing north of Scotland. She was the smartest chicken in the coop, she got A's in all of her Eggsams and went to study at St. Hendrews. There she would graduate with a masters in physics researching lasers that would help chicken's fly. The Chicken had learnt it all, or so she thought...
